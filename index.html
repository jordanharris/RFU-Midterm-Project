Tours

Tours are designed by you! At GiraNaut we care that the tour you get, is the tour you want. Our guides are highly knowledgeable and know their city better than anyone. Want a tour of the Eiffel Tower and the Luvre? Or maybe you'd like a tour of the best of Paris nightlife. You talk to the guides ahead of time and determine your perfect tour!

Guides

GiraNaut Guides are some of the best guides in the world. Each guide must go through a rigorous application process and is continually reviewed to ensure only the best guides remain part of the GiraNaut team. We take pride in our guides and know that you'll have amazing stories to share with your friends when you finish your tour.

Payment

At GiraNaut we want to ensure that payments for services are SAFE and EASY. Thats is why we utilize paypal and will never ask for your credit card information directly.
